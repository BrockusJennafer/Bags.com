Paper Pumpkins


What you'll need:
    1 Handleless paper bag
    Newspaper (if you have none, any kind of paper will do, you just want a filler)
    Black construction paper
    (Optional) Greeen construction paper
    Colored markers or pecils (if you wanted to be fancy, swap this out for paints instead)
    Scissors (a parent's help is advised if crafter is under the age of 12)
    Glue stick

What to do:

First you'll take your paper bag and your colored pencils, mark two inches from the top of the bag with green pencils and color that entire top with green, next the rest of the bag in orange pencils, open the bag up, and set it aside. Next take your newspaper and crumple up a few sheets until you have a good mass of filler.


Put this mass of crumpled newspaper into the paper bag, you want under half the bag filled with the mass, when done, take the top two inches of the bag and you'll shut the bag shut.


You'll twist the top of the bag next to give the pumpkin stem it's iconic stem look.


Take your black construction paper and one of the colored pencils, outline a jack-o-latern face, cut it out with the scissors. Do the same with the green paper outlining leaves and vine strans. Roll the leaves and vine strans to desires curliness. When done, take your glue stick, rub the stick on your desired positions on the paper pumpkin and place features.


You should be fully done here!
